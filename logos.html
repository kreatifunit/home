Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do I need to pay a deposit before work starts? ▼
Yes, a 50% deposit is required before we start working on your logo design. The remaining 50% is due upon completion when you approve the final design.
When do I pay the remaining balance? ▼
The remaining 50% balance is due after the design is completed. Once you approve the final logo, we will send you the final invoice for the remaining balance.
Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied with the design? ▼
Unfortunately, we do not offer refunds. We work closely with you throughout the design process to ensure the logo meets your expectations. Multiple revisions are available to achieve your desired outcome.
How long does it take to complete my logo design? ▼
The logo design process typically takes 5 to 7 business days. This may vary depending on the complexity of your requirements and the number of revisions needed.
What files will I receive with my logo? ▼
Once the logo is completed and the final payment is made, you will receive the logo in various formats depending on the chosen plan, including high-resolution PNG, JPG, PDF, and SVG (Vector) files.
Will I own the copyright to my logo? ▼
Yes, once the final payment is made, you will own the full copyright to the logo. It will be yours to use for any purpose related to your business or personal projects.
