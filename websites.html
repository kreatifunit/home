Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How long does it take to complete a website? ▼
The time required to complete a website varies based on the scope of the project. For example, a simple, static one-page website can be completed quickly, while more complex projects like e-commerce sites may take up to 14 days. We use pre-designed templates to speed up the process, but the exact timeline depends on your specific requirements.
Do I own the rights to the template or theme? ▼
The templates or themes we use remain the property of Kreatifunit Studio. You are granted a license to use the template for your website, but the copyright remains with us or the template provider. Customizations made to the template may be owned by you.
Are the prices one-time payments or are there any hidden costs? ▼
The prices quoted are for the web development and design of the website only. They do not include costs for domain name registration, hosting services, or ongoing maintenance. These additional services are billed separately.
Will you assist with domain registration and hosting? ▼
Yes, we will assist you in selecting the best hosting services and help you with domain registration. Additionally, we can guide you through setting up custom email accounts for your domain.
Do you offer backend or database-driven websites? ▼
Currently, we specialize in front-end development. This means we focus on creating visually engaging, user-friendly websites. We do not build sites that require user logins, real-time data processing, or complex databases. If you need backend development, we can recommend trusted partners.
