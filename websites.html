Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do I need to pay a deposit before work starts? ▼
Yes, a 50% deposit is required before we start working on your logo design. The remaining 50% is due upon completion when you approve the final design.
When do I pay the remaining balance? ▼
The remaining 50% balance is due after the design is completed. Once you approve the final logo, we will send you the final invoice for the remaining balance.
Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied with the design? ▼
Unfortunately, we do not offer refunds. We work closely with you throughout the design process to ensure the logo meets your expectations. Multiple revisions are available to achieve your desired outcome.
What is your design process? ▼
Our design process begins with a discovery phase, where we learn about your brand, goals, and vision. After that, we create initial concepts and refine them based on your feedback. We collaborate closely to ensure the final design meets your expectations and communicates your brand effectively.
How long does it take to complete a website design project? ▼
The timeline varies depending on the scope of the project. Typically, a logo design can take 1-2 weeks, while larger projects like brand identity packages or promotional designs may take 3-4 weeks. We will provide an estimated timeline at the start of the project.
Can I request revisions to my design? ▼
Absolutely! We offer revisions to ensure the design aligns with your vision. After presenting the initial design, we allow up to 3 rounds of revisions. If additional revisions are needed, we can discuss further adjustments based on the project.
Do I get the copy of my website files? ▼
Absolutely, with an exception to trial period, once the full payment is made, all website related files are send as a zipped file for future modifications.
What are maintenance fees? ▼
These fees include web hosting service provider and domain name provider, from which we exclude our monthly maintenance charges for any website adjustments.
