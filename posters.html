Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How long does it take to complete the design? ▼
Depending on the type of design, it may take 3 - 5 days maximum to get the job done. Take into consideration that a menu design will take a bit longer to complete compared to a poster.
Do you offer a printout? ▼
We are working on a system to include printout, currently all our designs are delivered digitally.
What files will I receive with my logo? ▼
Once the design is completed and the final payment is made, you will receive the design in various formats depending on the chosen plan, including high-resolution JPG, PDF, files.
How do I make Payments? ▼
For each design, a 50% deposit is required before hand, once the job is done you will recieve a demo design to review then make final transatction of remaining 50%.
